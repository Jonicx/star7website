About Us

Star 7 Technology began as a general works contractor and construction materials and machines supplier in April 2014. With almost a decade of experience, the group has undertaken many challenging projects and accumulated skills know-how and experiences in electrical installations, power system design, building design, real estate works and other constructions, project management services, building trades and related engineering works.

Our Vision:

To be a respectable building contractor and building materials and machines supply delivering beyond expectation, always.

Our Mission:

To procure project at competitive pricing, provide safe working conditions and deliver quality work within reasonable time frame.
